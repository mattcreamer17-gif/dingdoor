How we'd think about your stack

Most of what looks like an AI problem is not one. We apply a specific read to your stack, write up what belongs where, and hand you a scoped build plan that a single strong contractor can deliver inside the seed window.

60%
Traditional code

Provider CRUD, credit ledger, chat routing, geo-zip lookup, notification plumbing. The right call is making it boring and durable.

30%
Rule-based logic

Category-by-category pricing bands, refund eligibility rules, verification gates, matching priorities by county. Wants to live in one readable, auditable place.

10%
Where LLMs earn their keep

Parsing free-text requests into the right category plus urgency, flagging chats that are about to ghost, summarizing reviews into a trust score the matching rules can use.
